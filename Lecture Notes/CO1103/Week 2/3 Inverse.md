# Definition of inverse

Let *f*: A -> B and $f^{-1}$ : B -> A.

A function $f^{-1}$ is the *inverse* of a function *f* if: 

1. $f(f^{-1}(x)) = x$ for every $x \in B$
2. $f^{-1}(f(x)) = x$ for every $x \in A$
