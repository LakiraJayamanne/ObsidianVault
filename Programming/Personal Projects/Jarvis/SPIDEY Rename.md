# Naming — Spidey & Spiderbots

- **Name: Spidey** — no backronym, no meaning. Just the name.
- **Agents: Spiderbots** — when Spidey can dispatch sub-agents to handle tasks in parallel, they're called spiderbots.
- Subtle Spider-Man ref throughout. Intentional, not on the nose.
